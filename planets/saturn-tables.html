Find Your Saturn

If you don't have your natal chart, or have trouble reading it, you can easily look up the sign of your Saturn by referring to the table below. For example, if you want to look up the Saturn sign for a birth date of June 17th, 1976, note that Saturn entered Leo on June 5th, 1976 and entered Virgo on November 16th, 1977. Therefore a person born on June 17th, 1976 would have Saturn in Leo. Times given are ET. To see what Saturn means in the signs, see our Jupiter in the Signs article.
