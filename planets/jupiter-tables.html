Jupiter Signs

If you don't have your natal chart, or have trouble reading it, you can easily look up the sign of your Jupiter by referring to the table below. For example, if you want to look up the Jupiter sign for a birth date of June 17th, 1976, note that Jupiter entered Taurus on March 26th, 1976 and entered Gemini on August 23rd, 1976. Therefore a person born on June 17th, 1976 would have Jupiter in Taurus. Times given are ET. To see what Jupiter means in the signs, see our Jupiter in the Signs article.
