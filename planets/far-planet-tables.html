The Far Planets

If you don't have your natal chart, or have trouble reading it, you can easily look up the sign of your Uranus, Neptune, and Pluto by referring to the table below. For example, if your birth date is June 17th, 1976, the last sign listed for Uranus previous to this date is Scorpio (Uranus entered Scorpio on September 8, 1975); for Neptune, it's Sagittarius; and for Pluto, it's Libra. Note that these planets/bodies are very slow-moving and travel through a sign for many years. Times given are ET.
