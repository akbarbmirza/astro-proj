Mercury Signs

If you don't have your natal chart, or have trouble reading it, you can easily look up the sign of your Mercury by referring to the table below. For example, if you want to look up the Mercury sign for a birth date of August 12th, 1969, note that Mercury entered Virgo on August 7th, 1969 and entered Libra on August 27th, 1969. Therefore a person born on August 12th, 1969 would have Mercury in Virgo.

Mercury turns retrograde frequently, and this means that it moves through the signs in reverse during these periods. As such, you may notice that Mercury will enter one sign such as Aquarius, and then enter the previous sign, Capricorn with an entry of "Mercury Rx enters Capricorn". Simply find your birth date and time if necessary and then determine the sign position in the entry immediately preceding your birth date.
